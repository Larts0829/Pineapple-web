Pineapple Ripeness Guide

Unripe

  • Green, hard exterior
  • No sweet aroma
  • Leaves don't pull out easily
  • Sour taste

Tip: Leave at room temperature for 1-2 days to ripen.

Perfectly Ripe

  • Golden-yellow exterior
  • Sweet, fragrant aroma
  • Slightly soft when pressed
  • Center leaf pulls out easily

Tip: Enjoy immediately or refrigerate for up to 3 days.

Overripe

  • Dark orange-yellow color
  • Very soft to touch
  • Fermented smell
  • Bruised appearance

Tip: Best used in smoothies, baking, or frozen desserts.
