ABOUT

What is this app?

Our pineapple maturity detection tool helps you instantly identify if a pineapple is unripe, ripe, or overripe just by uploading a photo. It's fast, simple, and designed to save you time and guesswork.

Why does it matter?

Picking the right pineapple can be tricky. Unripe pineapples are sour, and overripe ones can spoil quickly. This app makes it easy to choose the perfect fruit, whether you're shopping, harvesting, or simply checking at home.

How does it work?

We trained a machine learning model using hundreds of pineapple images at different stages of ripeness. When you upload a photo, the model analyze the Pineapple in just seconds!
